WHY: The Diesel Dependency Crisis

Indonesia runs 5,200 diesel gensets across 2,130 remote sites. Electricity can cost 2–30× the national average and emits ~3 MtCO₂ annually. Gili Ketapang’s density, high CEPC, and water insecurity make it an ideal flagship for transition.

Indonesia's remote islands are trapped in expensive, polluting diesel dependency. Gili Ketapang Island represents this challenge perfectly: 13,000 people/km² density, paying IDR 3,000-50,000/kWh versus national average of IDR 1,444/kWh.

PLN's aggressive "dedieselisasi" program targets 100% renewable transition by 2030. Strong policy alignment creates bankable investment environment.

💰 Market Size

Gili Ketapang: 2,160 kWh/m²/year solar irradiation, surrounded by seawater for cooling and hydrogen production synergies.

⚡ Proven Technology

No R&D risk - combining mature technologies: floating PV, PEM electrolysis, fuel cells, SWRO desalination.

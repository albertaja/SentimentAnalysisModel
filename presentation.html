WHY: Diesel Dependency & Water Insecurity

In 2024, PLN operated ~5,200 diesel units over 2,130 sites. CEPC ranges 2–30× the grid tariff (IDR 3,000–50,000/kWh vs IDR 1,444/kWh), emitting ~3 MtCO₂/yr. Gili Ketapang faces unreliable power and costly water imports.
